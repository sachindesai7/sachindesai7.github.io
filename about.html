About Me

Hey there! Welcome to my portfolio. My little corner of the internet.

I'm Sachin, a 3D/2D animator, rigger & technical artist who's been bringing characters to life since 2006. I've had the privilege of working at some amazing studios — EA, Ubisoft Abu Dhabi — and I'm currently at Lila Games, Bangalore working on an exciting Extraction Shooter game.

Over the years I've animated beasts, wrestlers, Disney heroes, TV show characters and everything in between — from mobile games to DreamWorks TV series.

This site is where I share my journey, the projects I'm proud of, and the little (and big) things I've learned along the way. Have a look around, watch the reels, and if something catches your eye, say hello — I'd love to chat!

Enjoy the stay! 💫
