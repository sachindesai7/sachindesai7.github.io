AI Art Exploration

Outbound

Zombie hunter game concept — player navigates a constant-shooter vehicle through extraction zones, looting and surviving. Game progression includes vehicle upgrades, weapon upgrades, pilot unlocks and zone escalation. Two AI-generated art styles explored to find the game's visual identity.

Images — ChatGPT  ·  Gemini  ·  Claude    Video — Seedance 2.0

Style 01 — Wild Wild West

  • Semi-realistic cartoony western aesthetic
  • Iron Sheriff stagecoach-tank as the player vehicle
  • Dusty desert environments with warm amber palette
  • Undead outlaw enemies, wanted-poster UI framing
  • Key art, gameplay mockups, UI frames and app icon generated with AI

Style 02 — Dragon Hunter

  • Dark fantasy semi-realistic art style
  • Dragon Forge war chariot as the player vehicle
  • Volcanic, ruined fortress environments — deep red and ember palette
  • Dragon and demon enemies with arcane energy weapons
  • Key art, gameplay mockups, UI frames and app icon generated with AI
AI Art Exploration

Ball X Pit

Casual arcade game concept — player launches balls to clear waves of enemies and obstacles across increasingly challenging levels. Two distinct art styles were explored using AI image generation to define the game's visual tone and target audience.

Images — ChatGPT  ·  Gemini  ·  Claude    Video — Seedance 2.0

Style 01 — Gummy Blaster

  • Bright, candy-pop art style with vibrant gummy colours
  • Cheerful enemies — gummy bears, jelly blobs and candy creatures
  • High-contrast UI with bold outlines and playful typography
  • Targets casual players and younger audiences
  • Gameplay mockups, key art, store icons and UI frames generated with AI

Style 02 — Jungle Pixel

  • Retro pixel art aesthetic set in a lush jungle environment
  • Pixelated animals and tribal enemies with chiptune-inspired UI
  • Deep greens, earthy browns and warm highlight palette
  • Targets nostalgic and mid-core players who enjoy pixel art games
  • Gameplay mockups, key art, store icons and UI frames generated with AI
