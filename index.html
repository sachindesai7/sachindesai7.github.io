About Me

Hey there! Welcome to my portfolio. My little corner of the internet.

I'm Sachin, a Senior Technical Artist, Rigging Artist, and Gameplay Animator with 20+ years in games and animation. I've worked at EA, Ubisoft Abu Dhabi, and Lila Games, Bangalore, building production-ready rigs, Unity/Maya pipeline tools, and gameplay animation for shipped projects.

Over the years I've built Maya tools, FBX export workflows, LOD systems, game-ready rigs, Unity integrations, and animation systems for beasts, wrestlers, Disney heroes, TV show characters, and modern game projects.

This site is where I share my journey, the projects I'm proud of, and the little (and big) things I've learned along the way. Have a look around, watch the reels, and if something catches your eye, say hello — I'd love to chat!

Enjoy the stay! 💫
